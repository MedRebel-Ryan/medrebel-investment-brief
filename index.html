2 · The Problem

The bottleneck is never the AI model. It is the data infrastructure underneath it.

Fragmented clinical data destroys enterprise value.

Operational data is trapped inside legacy practice management systems and EHRs, formatted strictly for single-location transactions. Point a dashboard or an AI model at raw PMS data and you get noise, hallucinations, and compliance risk.

Multi-site groups almost always run a patchwork of different PMS and EHR systems, one from each practice they acquired or opened. The old way to unify reporting is to rip those systems out and replace them with a single platform. That "rip-and-replace" forces every clinic onto new software, triggering billing lags, credentialing bottlenecks, and staff friction that freeze the revenue cycle.

Without a clean-data layer
Raw PMS / EHR data → Unstructured & siloed → LLM query → Hallucinations & failed audits
With MedRebel in the middle
Raw PMS / EHR data → MedRebel cleans & structures it → Trustworthy, AI-ready data → Accurate answers you can act on
The MedRebel solution

A non-invasive middleware layer that extracts, cleans, and structures performance metrics with zero clinic downtime, zero billing lag, and zero staff friction. Extraction and reporting run in production today; building the clean-data path out to an enterprise-grade governed layer is part of what this raise funds.

4 · The Fractional CRO in Action

The difference between a report card and a growth map.

Our fractional CROs do not just deliver a dashboard. They deliver two concrete operational moves every month.

The lead leak · conversion
The signal

Location B's lead conversion dropped from 68% to 41% in a month.

The move

Your ad spend is working, but response time slipped to a 4-hour delay. Put a 15-minute callback rule in place this week.

Unbooked treatment · revenue recovery
The signal

$42K in diagnosed treatment is sitting unaccepted over the last 60 days.

The move

38 patients walked out without booking follow-up. Run this automated reactivation campaign today.

Provider productivity · RVU capture (PM&R)
The signal

One physiatrist is billing 19% fewer work RVUs than the group median, on the same patient volume.

The move

The gap is documentation, not effort: under-coded E/M visits and uncaptured EMG and injection procedures. Fix the coding and the RVUs, and the revenue, rise without adding a single visit.
